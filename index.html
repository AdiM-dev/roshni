About Us

Our Vision

Roshni is driven by the belief that everyone deserves care, opportunity, and hope. We work for the wellbeing of underprivileged communities by focusing on children's education, supporting those in need, and giving back to society in meaningful ways. Roshni means light, and our mission is to spread that light through compassion, action, and community support
